Write-Off
On now is our script-writing competition, the NFS Write-Off.  there are 2 categories you can enter:

  • The Lightweight category where participants are challenged to write a script in under 3 pages, while having no elements that would "break the bank."
  • The Heavyweight category has no restrictions and participants are encouraged to go all out in their scripts.

You can enter both, but only one per category. Submissions are accepted by email to either Collin or Seab. All submissions due 4/1/2022. Winners will be announced the meeting after (4/8).
