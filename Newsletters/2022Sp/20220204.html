February 4, 2022

Last Friday we meet via Zoom to kick-off our Cards Against Humanity Film Fest and beat our previous record of 22 individuals in attending an NFS meeting via Zoom with at least 27.

Cards Against Humanity Kick-Off
At the kick-off, teams reached across time and space with the power of live video conferencing to draw the cards that will act to build the prompts of their films.  A total of 10 teams drew this year, once again breaking 2020's record of 7 films.

Reach out to Collin if your team needs your physical cards.  Final cuts are due to Seab by Tuesday, Feb 15th.

College Movie Festival
College Movie Festival is confirmed this year and NFS is participating!  You can check CollegeMovieFestival.com for the most accurate and up-to-date info about CMF.

  • Kickoff: Wednesday, February 16, 7pm at Big Ash Brewery
  • Screenwriting and Pre-Production: Feb 16 - Feb 25
  • Production and Post-Production: Feb 25 - March 6
  • Initial Cut due by 11:59pm, March 6
  • One-on-Ones with the Pros: March 26 at Northern Lights Stages
  • Final Cut due by 11:59pm, April 3

Reach out to Collin or Seab if interested in participating.
