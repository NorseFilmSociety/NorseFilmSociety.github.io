February 18, 2022

Coming out of CAH and going into CMF, last week consisted of the College Movie Festival Kick-Off as well as the Cards Against Humanity showing.

College Movie Festival
This year NFS has a single team participating in CMF, The EMBers.  At the kick off Wednesday, it was revealed there would be a single required element this year: a common theme of "Family/Community."  If you have interest in participating, reach out to Collin.  Official rules and a schedule can be found on CollegeMovieFestival.com.

NFS "Placemaking" Project
Wednesday, March 1st at 6:30p, will be the first instance of NFS's series of 3 film showings around campus as a part of the Placemaking Program.  Come on out to NKU's Haile Planetarium in the Science Center for a free constellation tour followed by a showing of Star Wars: Episode V- The Empire Strikes Back and a discussion about the franchise and its impact hosted by Professor John Gibson.

Mardi Gras Party
Tuesday, March 1st from 12-2p in the SU Ballroom there will be a Mardi Gras party put on by APB consisting of Free Lunch and a chance for up to $100 in FLEX.  In addition, NFS will be tabling at the event, if you're available and would like to represent NFS please sign up here.

Homecoming Talent Show
Got a talent to showcase?  Audition for the Homecoming Talent Show here representing NFS!
