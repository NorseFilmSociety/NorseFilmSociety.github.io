January 28, 2022

This week in NFS we got some info regarding 2 upcoming film fests: our Cards Against Humanity Film Fest kicking off next Friday & Prof. Chris Strobel dropped by to talk about College Movie Festival (it's back this year!)

Cards Against Humanity Film Fest
NFS's 5th annual Cards Against Humanity Film Fest kicks off at this Friday's meeting!

  • Crew limit of 5 people
    • PAs and actors do not count towards this limit.
  • Teams will draw one black card and one white card to act as the prompt for your film!
    • You may redraw your white card one time if unhappy with it.
  • 8 minute time limit
  • Films are due Feb 15th
  • The showing will be Feb 18th @ 7:30p

Start forming teams soon!  If you cannot find a crew, you can drop a line in our CAH Discord Channel or reach out to an E-Board member.

College Movie Festival
College Movie Festival is confirmed this year and NFS is participating!  You can check CollegeMovieFestival.com for the most accurate and up-to-date info about CMF.

  • Kickoff: Wednesday, February 16, 7pm at Big Ash Brewery
  • Screenwriting and Pre-Production: Feb 16 - Feb 25
  • Production and Post-Production: Feb 25 - March 6
  • Initial Cut due by 11:59pm, March 6
  • One-on-Ones with the Pros: March 26 at Northern Lights Stages
  • Final Cut due by 11:59pm, April 3

Reach out to an E-Board member if interested in participating.
