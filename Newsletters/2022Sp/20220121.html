January 21, 2022

Welcome back returning and new members of NFS!  Last week marked our first meeting of the semester.  We watched your WTF films from over the break, looked forward to the semester, and got a tally of who was interested in purchasing a shirt.  Anyone who couldn't make it or missed the tally; if you want a shirt, please reply letting us know.

Cards Against Humanity Film Fest
Our 5th annual Cards Against Humanity Film Fest will kick off at our February 4th meeting.  At our next meeting, we'll go over the fest more in depth, watch some previous examples, and share stories of past experiences.

Homecoming
While there's been a lot of flux around Homecoming this year, NFS is participating as an organization in the Gold Division.  We're aiming to work on our organization banner tonight (1/27) so if you want to help make it, or have any design ideas relating to the organization and our theme (the 90s), join the discussion on Discord.  Friday morning, we'll also have a square window to design and paint at the SU, so drop some design ideas for that as well.
More aspects of Homecoming, including the Helmet decorating competition, will be discussed at tomorrow's meeting.
